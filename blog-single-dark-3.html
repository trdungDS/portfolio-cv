Ordering Ballooon Bouquets UI Design and Animation

  • 10 April, 2022
  • TranDung
  • Reviews

The first part of this video will focus on the design process for creating the Add to cart page. If you often work with e-commerce products, you probably know that every detail is important when making such interfaces. The goal of any merchant is sales. We will now analyze a case of ordering balloons where we added a little gamification.

Initially, we made this effect for the mobile interface and published the source code on our Github. But later, we used it in one of the web interfaces, which was successfully applied. In general, such things very well give the interface individuality. They do not slow down the actions of users but, on the contrary, induce them to perform a certain action. But every control must be in place.

This design course demonstrates how to create an “Add to cart” web page for a balloon ordering service. For ordering a product, we applied one of the techniques that affect sales conversion. The whole process includes working in Figma and After Effects for the animation of a special control for selecting goods quantity.
